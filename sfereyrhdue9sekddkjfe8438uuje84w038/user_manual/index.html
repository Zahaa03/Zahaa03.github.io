User manual

General

  • Keys have two important signs: a letter and an arrow.
  • Keyholes also have two signs: a letter and a dot. The letter must match the key.
  • Insert the key into the keyhole with a dot beneath it, with the arrow pointing toward the dot. The key will exit on the opposite side through a matching keyhole without a dot.
  • The sign `^` means something is fragile, be carfull.
