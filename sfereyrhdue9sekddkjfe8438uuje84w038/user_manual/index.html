User manual for the puzzle.

General:

- Keys have two importans signs on them. A letter and a arrow. - Keyholes have two important signs on them as well. A letter and a dot. Letter on the key matches the letter on the keyhole. Always insert the key into the keyhole with a dot beneath it with the arrow pointing towards the dot. The key exits on the opposite side through a keyhole also marked with the letter, but without a dot.
