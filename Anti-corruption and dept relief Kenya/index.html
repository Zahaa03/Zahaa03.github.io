Anti-corruption and Debt Relief Kenya

Corruption and unsustainable debt have long undermined progress and potential in Kenya. Our mission is to empower local communities, advocate for transparency, and relieve the debt burden on future generations. Together, we can build a fair, just, and prosperous Kenya for all.

About Us

We are a grassroots, non-profit organization in Kenya, dedicated to fighting corruption and securing debt relief for marginalized communities through education. Our organisation thinks the best way to fight corruption and dept is to educate the populatoion on how to manage their dept and sirculate their government to get fresh, not corrupted officials into office.

What We Have Done So Far

  • Educated 5000+ people on dept and corruption.
  • Made 23+ training outposts to reach the most remote villages.
  • Saved an estimated 5+ million USGD from the hands of corrupted officials.
  • Helped implement better long term projeckts into communities.
